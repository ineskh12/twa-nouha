Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I apply if I’m not from the same African country?

    Only african female cofounder that have the nationality of the selected countries can apply

  • Is participation in the program free of charge?

    Yes

  • I don’t have a co-founder, can I apply?

    Yes but it would be better if you have one

  • How many people per team can apply?

    2 people per team can apply

  • I have more than 2 team members, can they participate in the bootcamp?

    Only 2 team representatives will be invited to the bootcamp

  • Is my startup eligible if we have male co-founders or male team members?

    Yes, as long as you have a female co-founder.

  • Is my startup eligible if we have male co-founders or male team members?

    Yes, as long as you have a female co-founder.

  • Can I apply to multiple bootcamps?

    no, your application will only be processed once

  • At what phase does my startup need to be for me to apply?

    This program is designed for idea-stage startups.

  • Where are the bootcamps going to take place?

    The bootcamps are going to take place in Tunisia, Senegal, Ethiopia & Tanzania.

  • I'm a national of Tanzania, etc but I’am currently living in another country. Can I apply?

    Yes, you are eligible to apply to participate in the bootcamp of your country of origin if you can be present physically during the bootcamp dates.

  • My company is not legally registered, can I still apply?

    Yes
